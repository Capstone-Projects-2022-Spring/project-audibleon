About AudibleON

Audible ON, is a program, which is designed to auto detect gestures in American Sign Language (ASL) in order to enhance communication abilities for users that may have a need for non-verbal communication in their daily life. Non-verbal communication is an imperative part of life for those living with a hearing disability whether the impairment is mild or profound. Additionally, non-verbal communication has become widely popular in the use of technology for user convenience. Considering this, this proposed application seeks to detect and recognize ASL hand gestures for the purpose of communication. By utilizing computer vision and machine learning, a gesture can be acknowledged and understood, resulting in either written, visual, or audible communication.

Created by Cooper, Westburg, Lazzaro, Ahmed, Mercado, and Laubert (2022)
